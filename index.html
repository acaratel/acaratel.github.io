More about me

My current employment

I'm working at CERN ad Digital IC designer. I'm currently developing a Pixel-Strip readout ASIC for the CMS tracker with the capability to select the interesting events already in the sensor module. The main challenges are in the strict Power Requirements and in the extrimelly high radiation levels. In particular I have been working in algorithms studies, system modelling, RTL description, verification and IC phisical implementation and assembly. In the past I have been developing a general purpose control ASIC for high energy phisics experiments.

My PhD studies

I'm currently a 4th year PhD student at École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). I'm part of the Mycroelectronic system Laboratory.
